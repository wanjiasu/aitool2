Top 5 AI Trends to Watch in 2024

From multimodal models to autonomous agents, here's what's next.

By AIverse Editor on June 10, 2024

From multimodal models that understand images and sound, to autonomous AI agents that can perform tasks for you, the world of artificial intelligence is evolving at a breakneck pace. In this article, we break down the five most important trends that will shape technology, business, and our daily lives in the coming year.

1. Multimodal AI Goes Mainstream

For years, AI models were specialists: one for text, one for images. Now, models like Google's Gemini and OpenAI's GPT-4o can seamlessly process and reason about text, images, audio, and video simultaneously. This opens up incredible new possibilities, from real-time visual assistance to creating complex presentations from a simple voice command.

2. The Rise of AI Agents

Imagine an AI that doesn't just answer your questions, but actively works to accomplish your goals. AI agents are systems that can plan, execute multi-step tasks, and use tools (like browsing the web or using an API) to get things done. While still in their infancy, they promise to revolutionize personal productivity and business automation.
